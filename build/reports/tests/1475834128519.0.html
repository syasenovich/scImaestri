  • Daytona

    Daytona belongs to the Signorini&Coco family of brands. Founded in 1973, its manufacturing ethos is based on the dedicated pursuit of stylistic perfection, from the design of each piece and collection, to the meticulous manufacturing, painting, and assembly processes, through to the finely tailored upholstery and hand finishes.

  • Ulivi

    Ulivi was born from a love of details, the smell of leather, the charm of solid wood. Constant research into materials, finishes and new color tones imbues all of their pieces with timeless elegance, allowing them to live and dwell comfortably amidst both modern and classic surroundings.

    Explore Maker >
  • Serralunga

    Serralunga is renowned for pushing the boundaries of materials, technologies and aesthetics in order to create groundbreaking products that both inspire and delight. By combining color, height and proportion, Serralunga’s products communicate among themselves and with the space around them, creating new landscapes and architecture.

    Explore Maker >
  • Dema

    Dema was founded in 1962 in San Gimignano, the heart of Tuscany, with a very clear objective: the production of high-tech sofas stemming from ongoing design research and a focus on delivering both functionality and quality in every product. All of their production work is carried out in-house, allowing the company to keep close eye on the quality control process.

    Explore Maker >
  • Giovannetti

    Founded in the 1960’s, Giovannetti was an immediate hit on the international scene thanks to its innovative and well researched products that proved to be a perfect match for the experimental climate of that time period.
