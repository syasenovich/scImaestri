Horm

In ancient Aramaic the word horm means “sacred place, with right to asylum”. Since 1989 HORM has been the "sacred place" in Northeastern Italy where internationally acclaimed architects, designers and artists have been working side by side with local skilled craftsmen to create exclusive products that, while thrusting their roots into the territory and maintaining their Italian style, can communicate with the world. With its wide range of products HORM is able to create complete living environments that range from the dining room to the workspace, and from the bedroom to the living room. With a unique blend of simplicity, art and innovation HORM offers a home environment that is essential, unique, and exclusive.
