Technical Information

Structure made of high-quality multilayered beech wood finished with gold leaf and colored varnish accents. Seat internal structure made of high-quality multilayered beech wood. Padding made of polyurethane foam, upholstered with premium quality velvet. The stitching of the seat has been realized through a reverse zipper applied by hand. Seat cover is not removable. Seat height: 18.9. Seat depth: 15.7. For custom options, please contact our Customer Service Team.

Hodara

Hodara, founded by Vittorio Hodara, is a laboratory of intuitions, feelings and ideas transformed into objects. An alchemy based on a taste for simplicity and a thirst for uniqueness. Their products serve as liaisons that unite designers and master craftsmen with an exclusive selection of raw materials. The Hodara collection comes to life in forms of plexiglas, oak wood and soft leather, creating unusual combinations and technically improbable details. Endless possibilities lay before them for converting traces of emotion into substance, exploring the delicate interplay between transparency and reflection, solidity and void.

Vittorio Hodara

Vittorio Hodara has decided to realize a dream that he cultivated for years: to create the kinds of objects that he himself would like to find in an ideal space. Thus the Hodara brand was born. His collection is based on research of form, on removing rather than adding, and on striving for uncompromising quality. The vision of Vittorio Hodara stems from a culture of natural elegance, which is expressed through wonderful details, refined materials and ingenious lines.
