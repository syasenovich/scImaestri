Daytona

Daytona is a collection produced by Signorini & Coco, a family owned company founded in 1973 and dedicated to the pursuit of perfection from the design of each piece, to the meticulous manufacturing, painting, and assembly processes, through to the finely tailored upholstery and hand finishes. The company's designs are all distinguished by a seal of original craftsmanship, expertly and perfectly applied by hand. Their finishes are made exclusively with eco-friendly paints and colors, certified by their manufacturers, since the environment is yet another genuine value that must be passed on to future generations.

Leo Dainelli

Leo Dainelli, together with Marzia Dainelli, founded the Dainellistudio in 2007. Dainellistudio conceives, designs and creates spaces that express a style respecting and promoting fine craftsmanship, materials and colors that tell a story. They chose to locate their studio in the heart of Tuscany, so that they could remain in direct and constant contact with master craftsmen. This also allows them to observe nature and artistic heritage, where shapes, colors and materials play a key role in their identity.
